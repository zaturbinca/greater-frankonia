Imperial Regiments List

Detailed overview of the specialized military, administrative, and guard units within the Frankonian Empire.

Army

Combative Armed Forces

The army is the last stronghold of the Empire's defense and the primary instrument of its power. They are the backbone of Frankonia's defenses, the first line of defense against all dangers. They are the Empire's fist, the sharp edge of the Empire's military power, with the vital and difficult task of fighting the enemy head-on and taking territory.

They are primarily responsible for leading the way through combat, attacking enemy forces, dispersing opposition, and opening the way. Aggressive action is necessary to make way for subsequent imperial forces to operate. By overcoming initial resistance and securing the ground, the Armed Forces make way for the subsequent deployment of the Empire's forces of stabilization, such as garrison forces and forces of pacification, whose purpose is to consolidate power, impose authority, and bring conquered land under Frankonian suzerainty.

A characteristic of the Frankonian Military is the complete lack of any requirement to join. The Empire believes that participation in its primary combat force is available to anyone willing to fight. So, there are no qualifications of any sort to join up – no tests, no background checks, no experience necessary. The invitation to join is available to anyone, under any circumstance, at whatever time they report for service. This creates an ongoing flow of manpower, enabling the Military to remain at constant levels of preparedness to carry out the Empire's needs for defense and conquest.

Military Police

Enforcement Armed Forces

The Military Police is the backbone of the Empire's military organization and the foremost preserver of discipline and order within the Empire's ranks. The Military Police is the military's backbone of discipline in the Frankonian military, the first line of defense against internal disruption and disorder that may undermine operational effectiveness. The Military Police is the Empire's protectors within, the keepers of military order and law, charged with the critical and challenging mission of preserving discipline and security.

They are primarily responsible for enforcing military discipline, investigating infractions of conduct, commanding military prisoners, and directing the flow of materiel and forces in an orderly fashion. Their stringent enforcement of discipline is necessary to make all the forces of the empire operationally efficient as well as inwardly cohesive. Through the enforcement of stringent discipline and discipline within an organized system, the Military Police enable combat units to stay focused on the mission without distraction by internal conflicts, and occupation and garrison forces to exercise proper functions within secured zones.

Unlike the public recruitment of frontline soldiers, recruitment into the Military Police Regiment requires the exhibition of discipline, reliability, as well as obedience to imperial law. The Empire believes that the discharge of duties within the interior enforcement unit is only possible by those with the best possible standards of conduct and fidelity. Recruitment is therefore selective, following thorough background screening, judgment, and character evaluation, as well as an established service record of reliability. The prestige of service within this key regiment is only available to those who measure up to high levels of demand and are deemed worthy to enforce the law within the army. Selective filtration ensures that the Military Police Regiment consists of individuals of the required integrity and capability to provide discipline and stability, as well as to enhance the overall efficiency and reputation of the Empire's military forces.

Government

Civil Independent

The Government is the heart and soul of any underpinning authority of the Empire, and through it, all other institutions, particularly the military, rely on it. Think of it as the brain of Frankonia – the one that gives laws, administration, and strategic plans to keep the Empire stable and growing. As the ruling body, the Government has the chief political authority as well as administrative powers, charged with the responsibility of running the Empire and providing guidance in its overall direction. Legislative duties – meaning the creation of laws applicable within the Empire – overseeing vast territories, managing resources, and formulating policies both at the domestic and international levels fall under its jurisdiction.

Good governance on the Government's part will hold the Empire together and stable, with just enough momentum to keep moving forward. With these clear laws placed before everyone, things would run smoothly under the influence of the Government – whether in any respect of Frankonia: society, economy, or national security. The Government, however, is selective in distinguishing its own members, unlike the general military, where a simple willingness to join is sufficient. Service in the Government is primarily based on merit and loyalty and the passing of an application.

The Empire expects its officials to be skilled, but more so, the Empire sees them as loyal to the Crown and to the Frankonian cause. Candidates must demonstrate their loyalty. The whole process is slightly selective. Only the individuals who are perceived by the recognized authorities as the most intellectually gifted and honest will be granted the opportunity to uphold the law and enact the will of the Empire.

Royal Guard

Elite Guard Independent

The Royal Guard is the elite unit dedicated to the protection of the Empire's highest values. They guard the heart of Frankonia, the ultimate first line of protection from dangers to the Royal Family as well as to key imperial structures. They are the watchful sentinels of the Empire, the impenetrable bastion of internal security, with the vital and stringent task of guarding the Empire's supreme authority and most important sites.

Their primary responsibility is to remain ever watchful, keeping key locations safe, providing tight security to the Royal Family, and responding quickly to any peril that may threaten those under their protection. They must be steadfastly committed and ready to ensure continuity and stability to the leadership and functioning of the Empire. By offering complete security to the Royal Family and key buildings, the Royal Guard ensures that the heart of the Empire remains secure, allowing the military leadership and government to function without interruption.

Unlike the open enlistment of the regular military, membership within the Royal Guard is selective and strict. The Empire's belief is that service within this elite protection unit requires men of the utmost fidelity, efficiency, and integrity. Not everyone is accepted; only those who undergo rigorous screening, intense testing, and have an immaculate service record and behaviour are granted the privilege of joining these elite ranks. Only those who demonstrate unshakeable fidelity and reach the highest levels of excellence are given the privilege of joining, ensuring that the Royal Guard is composed of only the best-trained and most reliable men.

Special Forces

Covert Ops Armed Forces

The Special Forces are the Empire's finest, best-trained military resource, an instrument of precision power. They are the spear point of Frankonia, the first strike at risk calling for unorthodox solutions and occurring beyond the reach of conventional forces. They are the Empire's clandestine branch, the leading edge of its military power, with the vital and immensely difficult task of penetrating to the depths of enemy territory and performing vital missions beyond the reach of conventional forces.

They are tasked with executing high-risk, high-payoff missions deep into enemy territory or in danger zones. These include special reconnaissance, direct action assault, counter-terrorism, and irregular warfare missions. Their greater level of expertise, rigorous training, and independence or small unit capability are necessary to execute strategic missions, shut down adversary operations, gain vital intelligence, or kill high-priority target dangers. Executing these key missions, the Special Forces provide the Empire with a vital advantage, sometimes setting the battlefield before other forces enter or where other forces fear to tread.

Unlike conventional Armed Forces' public recruitment, Special Forces entry is competitive and challenging. The Empire knows that it needs those with unmatched staying power, mental resilience, specialist expertise, and impenetrable devotion to work within these elite forces. Consequently, entry is competitive, and individuals must go through a rigorous selection process, advanced courses, and demonstrate phenomenal talent and staying power under maximum pressure. The opportunity to be among these secretive ranks is only given to the very few who convince they have the rare talent mixture to perform under maximum pressure.

Mechanized Infantry

Combative Armed Forces

Mechanized Infantry is an essential part of the Empire's forces, providing much-needed mobility and covered fire. They are the mobile armor backbone of the Empire's armor power, serving as the spearhead of rapid assault and exploitation on the battlefield. They are the Empire's armored fist, the sharp land power cutting edge, with the crucial but difficult task of moving rapidly under fire and capturing objectives quickly alongside armor vehicles.

They are primarily responsible for delivering swift attacks, assaulting and combating from or near their armored fighting vehicles and infantry fighting vehicles. They are able to match the tempo of the movement of tanks, capitalize on breakthroughs through enemy terrain, and secure key positions before they are attacked by the enemy. Their ability to deploy quickly to fight from cover is necessary to carry the initiative of an attack to its conclusion and secure territory in good time. Engaging in combined arms combat, the Mechanized Infantry enables captured positions and objectives to be held by power and simultaneously.

Unlike the open recruitment of the regular army, entry into the Mechanized Infantry is selective based on both aptitude and training levels. The Empire knows that warfare and maneuvering from advanced armor vehicles require a combination of physical conditioning, mechanical ability, and controlled coordination. As such, entry is not universal; it requires the display of mechanical ability, completion of strict schooling on vehicle maintenance and operations, and proficiency in coordinated combat operations. The opportunity to join those mobile ranks is extended only to those with the skills and discipline to perform within an armored unit.

Royal Family

Leadership

The Royal Family is the heart and center of the Empire, the highest symbol of its power and right to rule. They are the crown of Frankonia, a living symbol of its past, traditions, and future. They are the leaders of the Empire, the main source of its identity and strength, with the important role of representing the nation and giving final direction.

While the Government leads and the military protects, the Royal Family rules. They make the final decisions on important matters, protect the Empire's traditions, and make sure the royal bloodline continues.

Their presence is needed to give the people one clear, trusted family that the whole Empire can follow. By holding the highest authority, the Royal Family keeps the Empire united and ensures its rule continues without end.

Being part of the Royal Family isn’t a choice or something you can apply for. It depends only on being born into the family or, in rare cases, marrying into it. There are no qualifications to meet or tests to pass—just being part of the family by birth or royal law.

The power and responsibility of leading the Empire are passed down through family lines. It’s not earned through effort, but given by birth and protected by tradition and the rules of royal succession. This keeps the leadership within the family, just as the Empire intends.
