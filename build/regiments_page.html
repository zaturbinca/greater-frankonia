Imperial Regiments

An overview of the specialized military, administrative, and guard units within the Frankonian Empire.

Army

CombativeThe main line of defense against all dangers. Armed ForcesPart of the main military structure.

The Army is the primary land-based force of the Empire, responsible for frontline combat, territorial defense, and large-scale military operations. It is the backbone of Frankonia's might, composed of disciplined soldiers trained for any combat scenario.

Enlistment into the Army is the standard path for new recruits wishing to serve in the armed forces, providing a solid foundation for a military career within the Empire.

Military Police

EnforcementUpholds discipline and order within the military. Armed ForcesPart of the main military structure.

The Military Police (MP) are the guardians of order within the armed forces. Their duties include enforcing military law, maintaining security at key installations, and ensuring the conduct of all personnel aligns with the Empire's standards.

Entry into the Military Police is selective and requires a tryout, as members are entrusted with significant authority and responsibility.

Government

CivilManages the civil administration of the Empire. IndependentOperates outside the direct military command chain.

The Government is the civil administrative body of the Empire, responsible for creating, debating, and enacting laws and policies. It manages the non-military aspects of the state, including diplomacy, treasury, and internal affairs.

Joining the Government requires a formal application process, seeking individuals with a keen interest in politics, law, and administration.

Royal Guard

Elite GuardProtects the Royal Family and key state locations. IndependentOperates outside the direct military command chain.

The Royal Guard is an elite unit entrusted with the personal protection of the Royal Family and the security of vital imperial locations. They are the most disciplined and loyal protectors in the Empire, handpicked for their unwavering dedication.

Acceptance into this prestigious unit is highly competitive and requires successfully passing the rigorous trials of the Royal Guard Academy.

ActiveThis regiment is operational. AcademyRequires passing the Academy. Join Discord for info.

Special Forces

Covert OpsUndertakes high-risk, special operations. Armed ForcesPart of the main military structure.

The Special Forces (SF) are the surgical instrument of the Frankonian military, tasked with carrying out the most dangerous and sensitive missions. They operate in small, highly-skilled teams, conducting reconnaissance, sabotage, and high-value target engagements.

Entry into the Special Forces is by invitation and a grueling selective process, open only to the most capable and proven soldiers.

ActiveThis regiment is operational. SelectiveRequires a selective process. Join Discord for info.

Mechanized Infantry

CombativeSpecializes in armored assault and support. Armed ForcesPart of the main military structure.

The Mechanized Infantry combines the mobility and firepower of armored vehicles with the flexibility of foot soldiers. This division specializes in rapid assault and armored support, capable of breaking through enemy lines and securing objectives quickly.

Joining requires a tryout to assess a soldier's aptitude for operating in a high-speed, high-stakes combat environment.
