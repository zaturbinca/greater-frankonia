Military Responsibilities

Key duties and roles for each rank.

  • Grand Marshal: Head of the Armed Forces.
  • Field Marshal: Second in Command of the Armed Forces.
  • Generals: Lead divisions, plan large-scale operations.
  • Colonels/Majors: Command regiments and battalions.
  • Captains/Lieutenants: Lead companies and platoons.
  • Sergeants: Supervise squads, enforce discipline.
  • Corporals: Lead fireteams, assist Sergeants.
  • Privates: The backbone of the army, execute orders.

Military Privileges

Specific authorities granted at each rank.

  • High Command (General+): Authority over all military personnel, strategic command.
  • Field Grade (Major-Colonel): Command over multiple units, host trainings.
  • Company Grade (Lieutenant-Captain): Lead small-unit actions, recommend promotions.
  • NCOs (Corporal-Sgt Major): Direct supervision of enlisted, lead drills.

Note: The Royal Guard division is supervised directly by the Emperor, Crowned Prince, and Royal Advisor, with a Director appointed to manage the division's day-to-day operations.

Government Responsibilities

Key duties and roles for each rank within the government.

  • Chancellor/Vice Chancellor: Head of Government, oversees all civil matters.
  • Directors/Secretaries: Manage government departments (e.g., Foreign Affairs, Treasury).
  • Councilors: Draft, debate, and vote on legislation and policy.
  • Registrars/Assessors/Clerks: Handle administrative tasks, record-keeping, and citizen services.
