The Frankonian Saga

Our history diverges. Our destiny is forged in steel and strategy. Understand the world of Frankonia.

Alternate History: 1946

Imagine a world where WW1 ended differently. No Treaty of Versailles, only an armistice and minor territorial shifts. Germany, unburdened by restrictions, experiences a massive industrial boom, especially in the Rhineland.

By 1935, the prosperous region of Frankonia declares independence, sparking a civil war that rages into the late 1940s. Our timeline is set in 1946, amidst this conflict, centered in the conquered Rhineland territories.

  • Ideology: Monarchy
  • National Animal: Bear
  • Setting: Industrialized Rhineland, 1946

The Battlefield

Operations center around a massive, fortified base within the Rhineland district. Outside lies an industrial city for civilians, and further out, a small town serves as the rebel spawn. The heart of the base contains the HQ and government buildings, marked by a replica of the Brandenburg Gate.
