Imperial Regiments List

Detailed overview of the specialized military, administrative, and guard units within the Frankonian Empire.

Army

Combative Armed Forces

The Frankonian Army stands as the Empire's primary military force, a formidable bulwark against any and all threats. Comprising the vast majority of our armed personnel, the Army is responsible for frontline combat, territorial defense, and large-scale offensive operations.

Soldiers in the Army undergo rigorous basic training, instilling in them the core values of discipline, teamwork, and combat effectiveness. They are the versatile backbone of our military, proficient in infantry tactics, fortification, and combined arms warfare.

Serving in the Army is a path of honor and duty, open to all new enlistees who demonstrate the will to defend Frankonia. It is here that a soldier's journey begins, with ample opportunities for advancement into specialized roles and leadership positions.

Active Enlist Now

Military Police

Enforcement Armed Forces

The Military Police (MP) are the guardians of order and discipline within the Frankonian military and its territories. This specialized unit is entrusted with enforcing military law, maintaining security at key installations, and ensuring the conduct of all personnel adheres to the highest standards.

MPs are selected for their integrity, composure under pressure, and unwavering commitment to justice. Their training focuses on de-escalation, investigation, and crowd control, making them a vital presence during both peacetime and active conflict.

Entry into the Military Police is selective, requiring a candidate to demonstrate exemplary conduct and pass a rigorous tryout process. They are the thin line that upholds the very structure and discipline upon which our Empire is built.

Active Tryout

Government

Civil Independent

The Government of Frankonia is the civil heart of the Empire, responsible for administration, legislation, and diplomacy. Operating alongside the military, this body ensures the smooth functioning of the state, manages resources, and shapes the policies that guide our nation's future.

Members of the government are involved in everything from drafting laws in the council to managing diplomatic relations and overseeing the imperial treasury. It is a path for those with sharp minds, a passion for statecraft, and a desire to serve the Empire through policy and administration.

Joining the government requires a formal application process, seeking individuals with strong communication skills, strategic thinking, and a deep understanding of Frankonian law and values.

Active Application

Royal Guard

Elite Guard Independent

The Royal Guard is an elite, hand-picked force charged with the most sacred duty in the Empire: the protection of the Rheinhold Royal Family and the security of the Imperial Palace. They are the silent, ever-vigilant sentinels who stand as the last line of defense for the monarchy.

Membership in the Royal Guard is a mark of supreme honor and trust. Guardsmen are chosen from the best of the best, excelling in marksmanship, close-quarters combat, and protective detail tactics. Their loyalty is absolute, and their discipline is unmatched.

To be considered for this prestigious role, a soldier must not only be an exceptional warrior but must also graduate from the highly demanding Royal Guard Academy, a trial that tests mind, body, and spirit to their absolute limits.

Active Academy

Special Forces

Covert Ops Armed Forces

Operating in the shadows, the Frankonian Special Forces (FSF) are the surgical instrument of the High Command. This unit is deployed for the most dangerous and critical missions, including reconnaissance behind enemy lines, high-value target extraction, and counter-terrorism.

FSF operators are masters of unconventional warfare, infiltration, and survival. They are trained to be adaptable, resourceful, and lethal, capable of achieving strategic objectives with minimal support and maximum discretion.

The path to joining the Special Forces is shrouded in secrecy and is incredibly demanding. Only the most resilient, intelligent, and skilled soldiers from across the Armed Forces are invited to undergo the selective process.

Active Selective

Mechanized Infantry

Combative Armed Forces

The Mechanized Infantry represents the armored fist of the Frankonian Army. This division combines the mobility and firepower of armored vehicles with the flexibility of infantry, allowing for rapid advances and devastating assaults on enemy positions.

Personnel in this regiment are trained as both skilled soldiers and proficient vehicle crewmen, capable of operating tanks, armored personnel carriers, and other combat vehicles. They specialize in high-speed maneuvers and breaking through fortified enemy lines.

Entry into the Mechanized Infantry is competitive, requiring a tryout that tests a soldier's aptitude for vehicle operation, technical knowledge, and ability to coordinate in a fast-paced combat environment.

Active Tryout

Royal Family

Leadership

The Rheinhold Royal Family is the hereditary leadership of the Germanic Empire of Frankonia. As the ultimate authority, the Emperor and the Royal Family are the custodians of the nation's traditions, the arbiters of its destiny, and the commanders-in-chief of all its forces.

Their role transcends simple governance; they are the living symbol of Frankonian unity, strength, and sovereignty. They make the highest-level strategic decisions, guide the nation's grand strategy, and inspire loyalty and valor in all subjects of the Empire.

Position within the Royal Family is determined by bloodline and is not attainable through military or civil service. It is a role of immense responsibility, passed down through generations to ensure the continued prosperity and security of the Empire.
