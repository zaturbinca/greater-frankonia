The Battlefield

The primary operational zone is a district within the contested Rhineland region, strategically designed for defense and control:

  • The Citadel: A massive fortification dominates the landscape, featuring formidable walls and defined borders. This serves as the central Frankonian stronghold.
  • The Industrial City: Situated outside the Citadel's perimeter is a bustling industrial city, home to the civilian population and vital production facilities.
  • The Rebel Town: Further out lies a smaller town, serving as the primary staging area for opposing forces.
  • High Command: Located inside the Citadel is an inner fortification housing the central headquarters, government buildings, and key command structures.

Control of these key areas dictates the flow and outcome of all engagements.
